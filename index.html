The Yaab story

Yaab Burgers was created to bring real East African flavour into the fried-chicken world. Familiar format, different energy.

Our in-house berbere sauce is built on carefully measured spice, fresh lemon juice, tomato, honey, black pepper, garlic and a mix of fats for body. Every batch is weighed and logged.

Built for repeatability

Behind the flavour, Yaab is all about clean systems: standardised recipes, portioning guides and a tight menu keep service sharp and waste low.
