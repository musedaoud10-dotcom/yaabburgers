About

Yaab Burgers brings East African berbere spice into the fried-chicken world. Familiar format, different energy.

Our in-house berbere sauce is built on carefully measured spice, fresh lemon, tomato, honey, black pepper and garlic. Every batch is weighed and logged.

Built for consistency

Standardised recipes, portioning guides and a tight menu keep service sharp and waste low. The goal is simple: every burger hits the same standard, every time.
