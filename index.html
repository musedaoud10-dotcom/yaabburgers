Berbere at the centre

Our in-house sauce starts with East African berbere spice, layered with lemon, tomato, honey, garlic and black pepper for heat, brightness and depth.

Thighs only

We use boneless chicken thighs – juicy, flavourful and strong enough to stand up to the spice. Coated and fried to order, never reheated.

Built for repeatability

Standardised recipes and portioning make Yaab ideal for dark kitchens and busy service, without losing the soul of the food.

Yaab Burgers was created to bring real East African flavour into the fried-chicken world. Familiar format, different energy.
