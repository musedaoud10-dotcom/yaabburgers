Our Story

Yaab Burgers brings East African berbere spice into the fried-chicken world, with a set-up that’s ready to scale from dark kitchen to high street.

Starting with a sauce

Yaab began with a question: how do you take the complexity of berbere and make it work inside a fast, modern burger kitchen without losing its character?

The answer is our in-house berbere sauce – built on carefully measured spice, fresh lemon juice, tomato, honey, black pepper, garlic and a mix of fats for body. Every gram is weighed, every batch logged.

Dipping crispy chicken thighs in that sauce straight out of the fryer brings everything together: heat, brightness, sweetness and depth in one bite.

A brand built on systems

Behind the flavour, Yaab is all about clean systems and repeatability. Standardised recipes, portioning guides and a tight menu keep service sharp and waste low.

That makes Yaab ideal for:

  • Dark kitchens focused on delivery.
  • Compact high-street sites with strong evening trade.
  • Partnership kitchens in pubs or food halls.

The aim: a recognisable signature burger that tastes the same in every kitchen we operate from.

Authenticity

We respect the heritage of berbere and where it comes from. The spice isn’t a gimmick – it’s the foundation.

Precision

Batch recipes, clear training and strict cook-to-order standards keep things consistent and safe.

Innovation

We’re not trying to be another southern-fried copy. Yaab has its own lane and its own flavour profile.

Memorability

The goal is simple: you remember the taste, you remember the name, and you come back for it.
