About Yaab Burgers

Yaab Burgers brings East African berbere spice into the fried-chicken world.

Berbere at the centre

Our in-house berbere sauce is built on carefully measured spice, fresh lemon, tomato, honey, black pepper and garlic. Every batch is weighed, logged and cooked the same way for repeatable flavour.

Crispy boneless chicken thighs are fried to order and dipped straight into that sauce, then finished with charred slaw, pickles and a toasted brioche bun.

Built for consistency

Yaab is designed to work in busy kitchens – dark kitchen, high-street site or partnership venue. Standardised recipes and a tight menu keep things sharp.

The goal is simple: you remember the taste, you remember the name, and you come back for it.
